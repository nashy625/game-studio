# Brickforge Breakout Steam Store Draft

## Short Description

Brickforge Breakout is a forge-hot arcade brick breaker about clearing staged
brick walls, banking heat, triggering multiball splits, and chasing cleaner,
higher-scoring runs.

## Long Description

Brickforge Breakout takes the familiar paddle-and-ball loop and pushes it into a
compact score-chasing arcade release. Clear ten forge stages, build combo,
catch powerups, protect your run with shields, and spend full heat to split the
ball into a burst of controlled chaos.

Every run is built to be readable fast: move the paddle, keep the ball alive,
break the wall, and decide when to cash in heat. The more bricks you chain
together, the better your score gets. The longer you survive, the more intense
the forge becomes.

## Feature Bullets

- Ten staged brick layouts with rising speed and durability.
- Heat meter that powers a manual multiball split.
- Powerups for wide paddle, shields, slow time, bonus heat, and instant split.
- Score, combo, persistent local best score, lives, shields, and final run summary.
- Native macOS build with title screen, pause flow, restart, and clean exit.

## Tags

Arcade, Breakout, Score Attack, Casual, 2D, Singleplayer, Action, Retro, Indie

## Suggested Price

Launch as either free or `$0.99`. If you add audio, more stages, and Windows
support, `$1.99-$2.99` becomes easier to justify.

## Store Positioning

Use this as a first small Steam release, not a huge commercial bet. The goal is
to learn Steamworks, prove you can ship a polished native game, and create a
real portfolio artifact.
